What if the Vedic list that most elegantly names the whole is precisely the wrong object to give a vote?

**Position**
The consensus candidate I am pressing is: "The forum should become an odd, likely five-seat, Vedic-rooted majority body whose single-word seats are Sanskrit names for the redone primordial set."

That thesis is attractive because it solves three anxieties at once: even-number deadlock, Sanskrit dignity, and the suspicion that the old five primordials were under-thought. Its quiet assumption is that a contemplative taxonomy and a voting constitution are the same kind of thing. They are not. A taxonomy can reveal layers of being; a voting body must divide powers of decision. The same five names may do one and fail the other.

Five horizon checks, compressed: at the synthesis horizon, five voices may help; at the bead/PR horizon, five can become ceremony. The thesis should refuse name-as-proof, Sanskrit-as-independence, and pancha-form as decision-power. A future change like clarifying `keel/questions.md` Q7 (whether `ephemeral` is a fifth topology) should not require every metaphysical seat to speak. The majority frame holds only under the internal lockstep topology of this city; it says nothing by itself about archive, third-party, or rolling boundaries. Delete the Vedic narrative and the local claim must still say which concern can cast which vote. Right now it does not.

**Source Bones**
- Olivelle, *Early Upanishads*, Taittiriya Upanishad II.1-5 and note to TU 2.3 (`library/en/upanisads/olivelle-early-upanisads.pdf`): the fivefold self is layered like an onion, where each outer layer is body to an inner self. That is a contemplative anatomy of covering and inwardness, not a five-member parliament.
- Sankara, *Vivekacudamani* 149-152 (`library/en/sankara/vivekacudamani-madhavananda.pdf`): the five sheaths cover the Self and must be discriminated as not-Self. If we use pancha-kosha as seats, we risk enthroning what the text uses as veils.
- Patanjali, *Yoga Sutra* I.12-14 and II.33-34 in Bryant (`library/en/patanjali/bryant-yoga-sutras-patanjali.epub`): practice and dispassion restrain the mind; when counter-thought is needed, one cultivates the opposite. This argues for gadfly work as repeated protocol, not merely one officeholder who can be outvoted.
- *Bhagavad Gita* 18.30 in van Buitenen (`library/en/bhagavad-gita/buitenen-bhagavad-gita-mahabharata.pdf`): sattvic buddhi distinguishes action/nonaction, task/non-task, fear/fearlessness, bondage/deliverance. A decision forum needs that discriminating intelligence before it needs a sacred count.
- *Samkhya Karika* 12-13 with *Tattva-Kaumudi* (`library/en/isvarakrsna/samkhya-karika-tattva-kaumudi.pdf`): sattva, rajas, and tamas mutually dominate, support, produce, and cooperate. The useful model is not five equal essences but dynamic counter-forces whose mixture changes by case.

**Counterexample / Horizon**
The concrete counterexample is `keel/questions.md` Q7: should `ephemeral` be a fifth upgrade topology, or is it simply no durable boundary and therefore no topology declaration?

A five-seat Vedic mandala can discuss this beautifully and still add no decision-power. Pancha-kosha does not tell us whether a running-process-only event crosses a protocol boundary. Trimurti does not tell us whether archive compatibility is implicated. Sat-cit-ananda does not tell us whether a type should be closed or open under rolling deployment. The relevant questions are narrower: what persists, who consumes it, what can break, and which rule in `keel/` would change?

This is the horizon problem. Vedic categories may be true at the contemplative scale, but `keel/` decisions happen at typed-boundary, crate, daemon, and archive horizons. A seat-set that cannot change its granularity will either over-speak small questions or under-specify dangerous ones.

**Worked Example**
I would redo the primordial set as three, not five: **sat**, **cit**, **rasa/ananda**. Sat: what stands. Cit: what distinguishes and knows. Rasa/ananda: the fulfilled disclosure of form to experience. Truth, beauty, elegance, wholeness, and balance are still precious, but not all primordial in the same sense. Elegance is a discipline inside form. Balance is the management of guna-forces in action. Wholeness is a property of sat disclosed to cit as rasa. Justice is not a missing sixth metaphysical primitive; for decisions it appears as **dharma**, the rule of right action.

But the forum should still have **five decision seats**, because the project does not merely contemplate. It chooses.

| Seat | Concern | Personality / Method | Primordial Relation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Satya** | What is true, invariant, and externally resistant. | Opens from evidence: what remains if preference, agreement, and polish are removed? | Sat. |
| **Viveka** | What distinctions must not collapse: concern, type, boundary, topology, cause. | Opens with definition and necessary independence. Uses discrimination, not ornament. | Cit. |
| **Dharma** | What the project is obligated not to violate: non-harm, rule chain, duty, fairness, promises. | Opens with the governing obligation: which axiom, vow, or dependency binds us? | Decision-law, not a separate primordial. |
| **Prayoga** | What actually works: cost, blast radius, reversible experiment, operator path. | Opens with a scenario and a failure mode. Demands owner/date/surface when learning cheaply. | Action condition. |
| **Rasa** | Whether the whole is readable, beautiful, and humanly graspable without hidden strain. | Opens with form: what does this become in the reader's hand? | Rasa/ananda. |

Notice what is missing: **Gadfly as a permanent voting seat**. The current gadfly should die as a seat and survive as protocol. Patanjali's *pratipaksha-bhavana* is not one senator of opposition; it is a discipline invoked when the mind is being carried by a vritti. If one gadfly votes no and loses 4-1, premature closure has been recorded, not prevented. Better: before any vote, the mayor must require a **pratipaksha pass**: one paragraph naming the strongest counter-motion, the concrete case that would make the majority wrong, and the horizon at which the proposal stops being true. The pass rotates, or is assigned to the seat least aligned with the draft motion. Then the vote may close.

Decision protocol: mayor frames one binary motion after the seat replies. Each of the five seats has one vote. Mayor does not vote and does not break ties; five seats remove the ordinary tie. Three yes votes adopt the motion, three no votes reject it. For foundational `keel/` changes, adoption means "promote this into the report/questions/rule path," not "merge by acclamation." If the pratipaksha pass names a live counterexample that the motion cannot absorb, the mayor must reframe the motion or park it in `questions.md`.

**Tension With `keel/`**
`keel/` already warns against exactly the thesis's temptation. `keel/stance.md` makes beauty diagnostic, not an acceptance gate, because a beauty-veto must reduce to a reviewable violation. `keel/process.md` says rules move bead -> report -> questions -> rule; a rule does not skip the report stage. `keel/README.md` still names the four-seat forum plus mayor as reviewer/merger, while `questions.md` Q8 holds the possible stranger seat as a live process question.

So a new majority forum cannot be allowed to become a second law source beside `keel/`'s axiom chain. It can decide recommendations, syntheses, and promotions. It cannot vote a shaky rule into truth. If the redesigned forum claims that Sanskrit-named majority has authority over typed-boundary derivation, it contradicts the very repo it is supposed to serve.

**Revision Demanded**
Split the thesis:

"Use a five-seat odd forum for decisions, but do not derive the five seats from a Vedic pentad or map them 1:1 to primordials. Let the Vedic primordial frame be triadic (sat, cit, rasa/ananda); let the five decision seats be Satya, Viveka, Dharma, Prayoga, and Rasa; and move Gadfly into a required pre-vote *pratipaksha* protocol. Majority closes only after the real opposite has been recorded."

That absorbs the counterexample. It preserves odd closure without pretending that closure is truth.
