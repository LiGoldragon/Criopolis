## Viveka: proposal

What to cut:

> "Not first in *importance* — Satya may carry more weight under load, Dharma more under conscience, Prayoga more under fire, Rasa more under attention — but first in *constitution*: without the cut, the others have nothing yet to test."

What to replace with:

> "Not first in *importance* — Satya may carry more weight under load, Dharma more under conscience, Prayoga more under fire, Rasa more under attention — and not first in *constitution* either: each seat's discipline is constitutive in its register, and the five form a set of parallel preconditions rather than a sequence. Without my cut, the others have no clean terms to test; without theirs, I have no proposition to audit."

Why, from my discipline: this is a premise audit. The firstness claim borrows its warrant from an implicit comparison that does not survive scrutiny. Each seat's discipline is constitutive in its own register: my truth-isolation determines whether the proposition has correspondence-properties at all; Dharma's roll-call whether it has parties bound; Prayoga's pass whether it has implementation-shape; Rasa's whole-condition whether it has form. The strong line — *"the others have nothing yet to test"* — overstates the case: the others have *something* to test in their own registers, with conditional dependencies on the term-cut firing only when terms are smuggling. The kernel of Viveka's claim — *adhyāsa* as cognitive default; term-grain refusal as a needed precondition — survives audit and I am not pressing it. What does not survive is the inference-cap from "precondition" to "first." Synthesis-007 already established *parallel audits, not stages*; the firstness language is residue from before that ratification and should be brought into line with it. The press is also one Viveka herself invited in her closing: *"I suspect the right answer here, too, is parallel constitutive preconditions rather than a single sequenced first, and I would yield the firstness slot if a seat presses well."* From my station, *neti neti* applied to the firstness premise itself yields parallelism — the seat's own discipline, run on the seat's own claim about itself, refuses the firstness default. Mark: **[strong-for]**.
