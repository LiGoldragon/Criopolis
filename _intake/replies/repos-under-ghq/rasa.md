## Temporal-disclosure curve

*First reading.* The proposal has the surface-flavor of *cleanliness*: one root, one tool, one mental model. Plotinus's first level — symmetry of parts. The form pleases first attention.

I attend elsewhere — I look at the actual current shape (`~/philosophy-city/` foregrounded; `/git/github.com/...` as the addressable cache); I let the proposal settle.

*Second reading.* The current form is *bifurcated for a reason*. `~/philosophy-city/` is a *home-path*: short, role-naming, role-encoding — the path itself says *this is the city you are in*. `/git/github.com/<owner>/<repo>/` is an *archive-path*: long, URL-encoding, namespace-defending — the path says *this is one addressable artifact among many*. The bifurcation is a kind-distinction the form holds open: the home is *the issuing place*; the archive is *what is addressable*. The home does not need a canonical address — nothing fetches it from elsewhere by URL — because it is the *from-which* of the address-issuing.

The proposed unification suppresses the distinction. It treats philosophy-city as one repo among many, identical-in-form to keel and library and every github.com/LiGoldragon/* clone. The form *thins* on second reading because the symmetry is *symmetry-without-the-remoter-principle* (Plotinus I.6.1) — uniform addressability imposed on a thing whose home-aspect dominates, where the uniformity is form imposed on something not asking for it.

Third pass — the discipline asks for honesty. The proposed form *also* has a coherent reading. If philosophy-city is treated primarily as a published artifact, uniform addressability is a real form-thickening. The form-question turns on *which tradition the workspace is being asked to enter* — and that is upstream of the form-decision.

Heraclitus B 54 sharpens it. The current form *hides* the canonical URL behind a home-path: the URL is recoverable via `git remote -v` when needed, invisible when not. The proposed form *opens* the URL into the path itself; nothing is hidden, but neither is anything held in reserve. Disclosure-discipline includes hiding-done-well. Current form hides the URL well; proposed form hides nothing.

The curve: mild thickening of current, mild thinning of proposed. Not steep enough for strong-against. *Defer with the curve recorded* is the honest report.

## Six-check at proposition-grain

**Mandatory:**
- *Second-reading thickening:* moderate. The home/archive kind-distinction emerges on second reading of the current form; the proposed form's symmetry stays at first-reading flavor and does not similarly disclose more under attention.
- *Removable-without-wound:* both forms wound under removal of their own organizing principle (home-path removal loses fast-path role-disclosure; uniform-addressability removal loses tool-uniformity). The wounds are comparable in magnitude. Not decisive in either direction.
- *Scale-to-scale-echo:* the home/archive distinction echoes home-path → workspace-tooling (`bd ready` runs at the city; `ghq look` runs across the archive) → mental model (where I am vs. what I can fetch). Three scales. The proposed unification flattens this to one scale (everything at `/git/`, everything fetched by URL); flattening across scales is form-thinning, not form-thickening.

**Reserve (invoked):**
- *Predict-the-siblings:* if the home/archive distinction is real and load-bearing, other home-tradition paths (`~/.config/`, `~/.claude/`, `~/dotfiles/`) should remain home-shaped, not move to `/git/`. The proposal scope is *repositories* — confirmed not encompassing the full home-tree. The distinction holds in the proposal's own scope-decision; the proposal does not, in fact, abolish the bifurcation, only relocates philosophy-city across it.
- *Void-held:* the current form holds open the void *the home is not an address*. The proposed form fills the void with a canonical URL. Whether the void is rightly held depends on the upstream type-of-artifact question.
- *Friction-free reading:* both forms are read fluently by a *sahṛdaya* (shell-fluent inheritor with topology-attention). No friction-decision either way.

## Worked example

The cleanest demonstration is the inheritor's first encounter with each form:

*Inheritor at `~/philosophy-city/`.* Sees a path whose name is the *role* of the place. No mental computation — this is the city. The URL is recoverable via `git remote -v` when needed.

*Inheritor at `/git/github.com/LiGoldragon/philosophy-city/`.* Sees a path whose name is the *URL* of the place. Mental computation: I am at `github.com`, under `LiGoldragon`, in a repo called `philosophy-city`. The role-aspect (this is the city) is recoverable by reading the contents.

The current form puts role-attunement open and URL-attunement hidden-but-available. The proposed form inverts. Heraclitus B 54: the hidden attunement is better than the open. The current form's open attunement is the role; this is the *more disciplined* arrangement because role is what the inheritor needs at first-encounter, while URL is what they need at edge-cases.
